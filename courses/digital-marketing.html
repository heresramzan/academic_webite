Digital Marketing Complete Course

This course covers modern digital marketing strategies. Learn SEO, Social Media Marketing, Ads, Analytics, and Email Marketing.

Lesson 1: Introduction to Digital Marketing

Digital Marketing is promoting products or services using online platforms. It helps businesses reach the right audience effectively.

Lesson 2: SEO Basics

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) improves website visibility on search engines. Focus on keywords, meta tags, backlinks, and site structure.

Build subscriber lists, send newsletters, create blogs, and write compelling copy to attract customers.
